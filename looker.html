LGU Dashboard Tutorial

A step-by-step guide to building the Service Request Dashboard in Looker Studio.

Phase 1: Connect to Looker Studio and Create Basic Charts

Step 1Create New Looker Studio Report

  • Go to lookerstudio.google.com
  • Click "Create" → "Report"
  • Click "Google Sheets" under "Connect to data"
  • Find your "LGU_Service_Requests" sheet and click "Select"
  • Click "Add" → "Add to report"

You should now see a blank canvas with your data connected!

Step 2Create Your First Scorecard

On the right toolbar, click "Add a chart"

  • Click the "Scorecard" icon (looks like a single large number)
  • Click anywhere on the canvas to place it
  • In the right panel under "Metric", click "Add metric"
  • Select "Record Count" - this shows total service requests

Let's style it:

  • In the right panel, go to "STYLE" tab
  • Change background color to light blue
  • Increase font size to 28px
  • Add "Scorecard title" as "Total Service Requests"

Step 3Create More Scorecards

Right-click your scorecard → "Copy"

Right-click → "Paste" (do this 3 times)

Arrange them in a row at the top. Update each one:

Scorecard 2
Metric: Click "Add metric" → "Completion Days" → "Average"
Title: "Avg Completion Days"
Scorecard 3
Metric: "Citizen Satisfaction" → "Average"
Title: "Avg Satisfaction"
Scorecard 4
Metric: "Cost Incurred" → "Sum"
Title: "Total Cost"

You now have 4 key metrics at the top!

Step 4Create a Bar Chart - Service Types

  • Click "Add a chart" → "Bar chart"
  • Click below your scorecards to place it
  • In right panel under "Dimension": Click "Add dimension" → "Service Type"
  • Under "Metric": Ensure "Record Count" is selected
  • In "STYLE" tab:
    • Chart title: "Service Requests by Type"
    • Show data labels: ON
    • Change colors if desired

Step 5Create a Pie Chart - Status Distribution

  • Click "Add a chart" → "Pie chart"
  • Place it next to the bar chart
  • Dimension: "Status"
  • Metric: "Record Count"
  • Style:
    • Title: "Request Status Distribution"
    • Donut: 40%
    • Show data labels: ON

Your dashboard should now have basic charts!

Phase 2: Add Interactive Filters

Step 6Add Department Dropdown Filter

  • Click "Add a control" → "Dropdown list"
  • Click at the very top of your dashboard
  • In right panel under "Control field": Select "Department"
  • Under "STYLE":
    • Label: "Select Department"
    • Width: 200px

Step 7Add Date Range Filter

  • Click "Add a control" → "Date range"
  • Place it next to the department filter
  • Control field: "Date"
  • Default date range: "Last 30 days"
  • Style: Width 250px

Step 8Add Checkbox Filter for Status

  • Click "Add a control" → "Checkbox"
  • Place it below the other filters
  • Control field: "Status"
  • Default selection: All checked
  • Style:
    • Label: "Filter by Status"
    • Orientation: Horizontal

Test your filters! Click different departments and dates - watch the charts update.

Phase 3: Create Calculated Fields

Step 9Create Efficiency Score Field

In the right panel, click "Resource" → "Manage added data sources"

Find "LGU_Service_Requests" and click "EDIT"

Click "ADD A FIELD" in top right

Field name: "Efficiency Score"

Formula: Copy and paste this EXACTLY:

CASE
  WHEN `Completion Days` <= 2 THEN "Excellent"
  WHEN `Completion Days` <= 4 THEN "Good" 
  WHEN `Completion Days` <= 6 THEN "Average"
  ELSE "Needs Improvement"
END

Click "SAVE"

Step 10Create Priority Weight Field

Click "ADD A FIELD" again

Name: "Priority Weight"

CASE
  WHEN Priority = "High" THEN 3
  WHEN Priority = "Medium" THEN 2
  WHEN Priority = "Low" THEN 1
  ELSE 0
END

Click "SAVE"

Step 11Create Weighted Satisfaction Field

"ADD A FIELD"

Name: "Weighted Satisfaction"

Citizen Satisfaction * Priority Weight

Click "SAVE"

Close the data source editor - you'll see these new fields in your field list!

Step 12Use Your Calculated Fields

Create a new bar chart

  • Dimension: "Efficiency Score"
  • Metric: "Record Count"
  • Title: "Service Efficiency Distribution"

Phase 4: Connect and Blend Budget Data

Step 13Add Budget Data Source

  • Click "Resource" → "Manage blended data"
  • Click "Add a data source"
  • Select "Google Sheets"
  • Choose your "LGU_Budget" sheet
  • Click "ADD" → "ADD TO REPORT"

Step 14Create Data Blend

In "Manage blended data", click "Create Blend"

Left Table
Select "LGU_Service_Requests"
Metrics: Record Count, Average Completion Days
Right Table
Select "LGU_Budget"
Metrics: Budget Allocated, Budget Spent

Join keys: Click "Add key" → Select "Department" from both tables

Name this blend: "Department Performance"

Click "SAVE"

Step 15Create Blended Chart

  • Click "Add a chart" → "Combo chart"
  • Place it below your existing charts
  • Data source: Select "Department Performance" (your new blend)
  • Dimension: "Department"
  • Metrics:
    • Budget Allocated (Bar)
    • Budget Spent (Bar)
    • Record Count (Line, secondary axis)
  • Style:
    • Title: "Budget vs Service Volume by Department"
    • Dual axis: ON
